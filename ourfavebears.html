Our Personal Favorite Bears

747

We like 747 for his floppy ear. Furthermore, no one can deny that he is an absolute unit, which is why, even though he faced an unfair challenge in the form of fat bear fraud, he weathered it and was crowned fat bear champion of 2022.

32 Chunk

We like 32 Chunk because he is one of the most dominant bears who will fight for his resources (especially that salmon). 32 Chunk is also astonishing because of his ability to carry fat reserves even when he is at his leanest.
Check out 32 Chunk's dominance in action!!!

854 Divot

854 Divot is one of our favorite bears because of her beautiful fur and how it changes color thorughout the seasons, going from tan in summer to an eventual rich, brown winter coat.
