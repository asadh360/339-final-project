What is Fat Bear Week?

Fat Bear Week is an annual competition to choose the fattest bear of the year!

Some of the largest brown bears on Earth make their home at Brooks River in Katmai National Park, Alaska. Brown bears get fat to survive and Fat Bear Week is an annual tournament celebrating their success in preparation for winter hibernation.

For each match-up of this single elimination tournament, you get to vote for the bear you believe best exemplifies fatness. Only one will be crowned champion of Fat Bear Week.

For extra fun, download your bracket to predict your own fat bear winner.
